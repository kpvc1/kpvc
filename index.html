The Process

Discovery

What is the goal of your brand? Where do you see your brand 10 years from now? What message would yo like to send? Do you have any design preferences? What is the timeline for completion?

Proposal

A detailed proposal is created that outlines the project scope, completion date, deliverables, and an estimated cost of the project. This collaborative stage aligns client goals and objectives with creative design strategy.

Research

This is the critical thinking phase of the process where thegoal is the conceptualize a marketing strategy. Who are your competitors? Who is your target audience?What are they looking for? How can we capture their attention?

Design

This is where concepts are formed. Sketches are drawn, mockupsand wireframes are designed, and tools such as Figma and Adobe are used to turn concepts into reality.You'll be a part of the design process and will be able to review and request revisions.

Development

Designs are polished and websites are staged. A focus on ensuring consistency with the project's goals and user experience are met, resulting in a cohesive, user-friendly final product and satsfied client.

Deployment

The final product, whether a t-shirt design, brand rollout or website is moved from the testing environment to the production. It includes setting up hosting, configuring databases, ensuring proper fit or functionality, and ensuring performance, security, expectations are met.
